Keep your furry friend secure, comfortable and happy in the Furry Lift Pet Carrier. Some features include a durable machine washable 100% cotton canvas that can be easily washed, and a removable fleece padding for comfort, easy removal and cleaning.

Efficient size 13"L x 8"W x 16"H fits comfortably under seats while traveling on the bus or train.

Our tote has an integrated 23 inch adjustable sewn in leash to keep your pet secure and ensure ease of movement. Padded insulated walls will guard your pet from impact, while an invisible zipper creates a secure interior pocket and ensures prying paws don’t touch your possessions.
