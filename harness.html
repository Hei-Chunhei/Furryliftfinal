* SUPERIOR DESIGN & BETTER CONTROL:


When your dog gets excited they tend to pull. During these times you are going to need an escape proof harness with maximum control. We included a back handle in the event you need to hold your pet more securely. Furthermore, our no pull design utilizes a sturdy metal leash attachment point and heavy padded material to help distribute pressure evenly to ensure your pet is comfortable while effortlessly giving you better control.

* EASY ON EASY OFF PLUS COMFORT:


The soft and breathable padding will fit snug around your furry friend. Your dog will feel secure and comfortable while wearing it and this will make your walks more pleasant. In addition, adjustable slide straps create a custom fit. These slide straps also make it very easy to remove and equip the harness.
